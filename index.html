How We Work

  1. Inquiry → You request samples (Standard & Premium).
  2. Samples → We ship from Sweden.
  3. Golden Sample → You approve exact reference quality.
  4. Order & Deposit → PO + 30% deposit (FOB Berbera or CIF on request).
  5. Sorting & Packing → Hand-sorted to match the Golden Sample.
  6. Video Proof → Weighing, labeling, packaging + Batch ID.
  7. Export → Via Berbera Port, Somaliland. Balance before shipment.

Transparency & Traceability

  • Video proof before export (weighing, labeling, packaging).
  • Batch ID on every shipment for traceability.
  • Family-based connections at Berbera Port for trusted, direct sourcing.
  • Hand-sorted quality; consistent aroma profile.
