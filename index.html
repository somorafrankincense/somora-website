How We Work

  1. Inquiry → You request samples (Standard & Premium).
  2. Samples → We ship from Stockholm.
  3. Golden Sample → You approve reference quality.
  4. Order & Deposit → PO + 30% deposit (FOB Berbera or CIF on request).
  5. Sorting & Packing → Hand-sorted to match the Golden Sample.
  6. Video Proof → Weighing, labeling, packaging + Batch ID.
  7. Export → Via Berbera Port, Somaliland. Balance before shipment.

About Us

Somora Frankincense is a family-based exporter of Somali Maydi (Boswellia Frereana). We ensure transparency and consistent quality through direct sourcing, video-verified batches and trusted port contacts in Berbera.
