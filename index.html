How We Work

Simple, transparent and buyer-friendly.

  1. Inquiry — You request samples (Standard & Premium).
  2. Samples — We ship from Stockholm.
  3. Golden Sample — You approve exact reference quality.
  4. Order & Deposit — PO + 30% deposit (FOB Berbera or CIF on request).
  5. Sorting & Packing — Hand-sorted to match the Golden Sample.
  6. Video Proof — Weighing, labeling, packaging + unique Batch ID for traceability.
  7. Export — Via Berbera Port, Somaliland. Balance due before shipment.
