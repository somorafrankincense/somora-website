How We Work

  1. Inquiry → You request samples (Standard & Premium).
  2. Samples → We ship from Somalia.
  3. Golden Sample → You approve exact reference quality.
  4. Order & Deposit → PO + 30% deposit (FOB Berbera or CIF on request).
  5. Sorting & Packing → Hand-sorted to match the Golden Sample.
  6. Video Proof → Weighing, labeling, packaging + Batch ID.
  7. Export → Via Berbera Port, Somaliland. Balance before shipment.

Transparency & Traceability

  • Video proof before export (weighing, labeling, packaging).
  • Batch ID on every shipment for traceability.
  • Family-based connections at Berbera Port for trusted, direct sourcing.
  • Hand-sorted quality; consistent aroma profile.

About

Somora Frankincense is a family-based exporter focused on authentic Somali Maydi (Boswellia Frereana). Our model is simple and transparent: we agree on a Golden Sample, then hand-sort every shipment to match it. Before export, we provide video proof of weighing, labeling and packaging, and we assign a Batch ID for traceability.

We work closely with families and handlers connected to the Berbera Port corridor. This allows us to keep sourcing direct, verify quality at each step, and maintain a consistent aroma profile shipment after shipment. We serve incense brands, essential oil producers, and specialty retailers who value reliability and a clear audit trail from origin to export.
