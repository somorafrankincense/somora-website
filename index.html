About Us

Somora Frankincense is a family-based exporter of authentic Somali Maydi (Boswellia Frereana) — a resin celebrated for its sweet aroma and purity. Our mission is to connect international buyers directly to trusted Somali harvesters, ensuring transparency, traceability, and fair trade from start to finish.

We operate between Somalia and Sweden, providing smooth export logistics from Berbera Port with verified video proof for every shipment.
